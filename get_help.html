Need Assistance? We're Here to Help

FutureTech Academy is committed to supporting students in every way possible. If you need academic, financial, or personal assistance, explore the resources below.

1. Academic Support

We offer various academic resources to help students succeed, including:

  • Free tutoring services
  • Study groups and peer mentorship
  • Access to online learning materials
  • Advising for course selection and academic planning

2. Student Success Coaches

Our Student Success Coaches provide personalized support to help you stay on track academically and personally. They assist with:

  • Setting academic and career goals
  • Developing effective study habits
  • Time management and work-life balance
  • Connecting with campus resources

Email: successcoach@futuretechacademy.edu

Phone: (123) 456-7892

3. Financial Assistance

Students facing financial difficulties can explore:

  • Financial Aid Programs
  • Work-Study Opportunities
  • Student Emergency Fund
  • Payment plan options for tuition

4. Mental Health & Wellness

Your well-being is important. We offer:

  • Counseling services (virtual and in-person)
  • Wellness workshops and stress management programs
  • Support groups for students dealing with personal challenges

5. Technical Support

For assistance with online portals, course materials, or other technical issues, contact:

Email: techsupport@futuretechacademy.edu

Phone: (123) 456-7890

6. Contact Student Support

If you're not sure where to go for help, our student support team is ready to assist you.

Email: support@futuretechacademy.edu

Phone: (123) 456-7891
