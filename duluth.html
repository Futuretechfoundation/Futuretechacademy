FutureTech Academy - Duluth Campus

Welcome to the Duluth Campus

FutureTech Academy's Duluth Campus is a hub for innovation in space technology, quantum computing, and AI research.

Campus Highlights

  • Specialized labs for quantum computing and space exploration technologies.
  • Cutting-edge humanoid robotics and BCI research facilities.
  • Partnerships with aerospace and AI companies for real-world experience.
  • Student innovation center with startup incubation support.

Programs Offered

  • Future of Humans Track – Advancements in biomedicine, prosthetics, and AI-driven healthcare.
  • BCI & Humanoids Track – Neural interfaces, robotic integration, and AI-driven cognition.
  • Quantum & Space Tech Track – Space propulsion systems, quantum computing, and UAP research.
