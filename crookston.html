FutureTech Academy - Crookston Campus

Welcome to the Crookston Campus

FutureTech Academy's Crookston Campus offers cutting-edge education, advanced research opportunities, and a vibrant student community.

Campus Highlights

  • State-of-the-art research labs for biotechnology, AI, and quantum computing.
  • Dedicated spaces for hands-on learning in brain-computer interfaces and humanoid robotics.
  • Collaborative workspaces for student innovation and entrepreneurship.
  • Modern student housing, dining, and recreation facilities.

Programs Offered

  • Future of Humans Track – Biotech, nanorobotics, and regenerative medicine.
  • BCI & Humanoids Track – Brain-computer interfaces, AI, and robotics.
  • Quantum & Space Tech Track – Quantum computing, space technology, and UAP studies.
