Parking & Transportation

FutureTech Academy offers multiple parking and transportation options to ensure easy access to campus.

Parking Information

  • Student Parking: Available in Lots A & B. Free with a student parking permit.
  • Visitor Parking: Located near the main entrance. Paid parking available at $5/day.
  • Faculty & Staff Parking: Reserved spots available with a faculty permit.
  • Electric Vehicle Charging: Charging stations are located in Lot C.

Transportation Options

  • Public Transit: Bus Route 25 stops in front of the campus.
  • Bicycle Parking: Bike racks are available near the main entrance.
  • Ride-Share Services: Designated drop-off and pick-up zones near the main gate.
