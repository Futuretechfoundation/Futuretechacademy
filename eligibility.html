Eligibility & Funding Information

FutureTech Academy offers various funding opportunities to eligible students, ensuring access to high-quality education. Below, you will find information about the eligibility criteria and available funding sources.

General Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be enrolled as a student at FutureTech Academy.
  • Must maintain satisfactory academic progress (SAP) as per institutional guidelines.
  • Must demonstrate financial need for certain types of aid.
  • Must not have any outstanding financial holds with the institution.
  • Additional requirements may apply depending on the specific funding program.

Available Funding Options

1. Scholarships

Merit-based and need-based scholarships are available to students who meet the criteria. Scholarships do not need to be repaid.

2. Grants

Grants are need-based financial aid that does not require repayment. Students must complete the required financial aid applications to be considered.

3. Work-Study Program

Eligible students can work part-time while studying to help cover educational expenses. Positions are available both on-campus and off-campus.

4. Student Loans

FutureTech Academy offers low-interest student loan options for those who need additional financial support. Loan repayment terms vary.

5. Emergency Funds

Students experiencing financial hardships can apply for emergency funds to assist with essential expenses such as housing, food, and medical needs.

How to Apply for Funding

Students interested in financial aid must complete the necessary application forms, provide supporting documents, and meet submission deadlines. For detailed application instructions, visit the Financial Aid Office or email financialaid@futuretechacademy.edu.
