Financial Aid Application Timeline

Follow this timeline to stay on track with your financial aid process.

  • Step 1: Submit FAFSA

    Deadline: January 15

    Submit your Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) to apply for federal and state financial aid. Make sure to include the FutureTech Academy school code.

  • Step 2: Financial Aid Package Review

    Notification: February 15

    After submitting your FAFSA, you will receive a financial aid package that outlines the aid available to you, including scholarships, grants, and loans.

  • Step 3: Accept Financial Aid Offer

    Deadline: March 1

    Review and accept the financial aid offer through your FutureTech Academy account. Ensure all required documents are submitted by the deadline.

  • Step 4: Disbursement of Funds

    Disbursement Date: June 1

    Once you have accepted your aid, funds will be disbursed to your student account to cover tuition and fees. Any remaining funds will be issued to you via check or direct deposit.

  • Step 5: Final Review

    Deadline: June 15

    Ensure that all financial aid documents are finalized and that your student account reflects all payments, credits, and disbursements.
