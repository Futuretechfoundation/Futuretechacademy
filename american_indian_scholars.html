American Indian Scholars Program

FutureTech Academy is proud to support and empower American Indian students through the American Indian Scholars Program. This program offers a unique opportunity for students to receive educational support, mentorship, and resources to succeed in their academic and professional journeys.

Program Overview

The American Indian Scholars Program is designed to provide American Indian students with the tools, resources, and networks they need to excel in their academic careers. This program offers financial aid, mentorship, and leadership opportunities, helping students achieve their educational goals and pursue successful careers in technology and innovation.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be an enrolled member of a federally recognized American Indian tribe or Alaskan Native group.
  • Must be admitted to or currently enrolled at FutureTech Academy.
  • Must demonstrate academic achievement or potential.
  • Must demonstrate a commitment to contributing to the American Indian community.

Program Benefits

  • Scholarships and financial aid support.
  • Access to exclusive mentorship opportunities with leaders in technology and innovation.
  • Participation in cultural and professional development events.
  • Internship and work-study opportunities in cutting-edge technology fields.
  • Networking opportunities with fellow scholars and industry professionals.

How to Apply

Interested students can apply by completing the application form on our admissions page and submitting the required documents. The application deadline for the upcoming academic year is July 15th.
